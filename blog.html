Thoughts on Assignment 1

9/24/2018

What did I struggle with?

My biggest struggle with this assignment was designing a site that I felt looked good, without using CSS at all. This served as an interesting challenge to overcome, and I mostly just spent my time making sure the spacing and text sizes were as good as I could make them. In addition, I struggled with the content itself. I've always struggled with finding "and interesting fact/story" to talk about, and it took me a while to think about a topic to write about.

What did I learn?

I mostly knew everything I needed to when I worked on this assignment. This included basic HTML structure and text tags, as well as the img tag and how that works.

What resources were most helpful?

I didn't really use any resources in completing this assignment, just prior knowledge.
